Microbiome Ecology & Functional Engineering Lab

at Yonsei University, Systems Biology Department  (PI: Kiseok Lee)

We study how to design robust microbiome function — combining microbial ecology, data-driven modeling, and synthetic biology to engineer microbial communities that perform reliably across changing environments, from agricultural soils to the human body.

The lab opens in March 2027. We are recruiting our founding team — see open positions.
