Help build a new lab

The MEFE Lab opens at Yonsei University in March 2027. We're recruiting our founding team. We welcome people from ecology, microbiology, engineering, physics, computer science, and beyond — what matters is curiosity and care.

Open Positions

Who we're looking for

Ph.D. & Master's students

Apply through the Department of Systems Biology graduate program at Yonsei. Backgrounds in biology, microbiology, ecology, bioengineering, statistics, physics, or CS are all welcome. Tell us what questions excite you.

Postdoctoral researchers

For researchers ready to lead a direction in (a) soil/rice-paddy greenhouse-gas ecology or (b) synthetic-biology engineering of host-associated microbiomes. Experience in anaerobic culturing, metagenomics, modeling, or synthetic biology is a plus.

Undergraduate researchers

Yonsei undergraduates can join for hands-on projects at the bench, in code, or both. A great way to find out whether research is for you.

Visiting researchers & collaborators

We actively collaborate across institutions and disciplines. If our questions overlap with yours, reach out.

How to apply

Get in touch

When you email, please include:

  • A short note on why you're interested in the MEFE Lab and which research direction appeals to you
  • Your CV (and transcript, for student applicants)
  • For postdocs: a brief description of your research experience and goals

Mentoring philosophy

Research and teaching are fundamentally social activities. The PI mentors by aligning each person's intrinsic motivation with the lab's questions, encouraging members to pursue the problems they find genuinely interesting — in a supportive, collaborative environment.
