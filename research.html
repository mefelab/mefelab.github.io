Research

Ecological engineering of robust microbiome function.

We study how to design robust microbiome function — metabolic function that stays reliable as environments change — by combining microbial ecology, modeling, and synthetic biology, across soil/rice-paddy greenhouse-gas systems and the vaginal microbiome.

Representative work: Functional regimes define soil microbiome response to environmental change (Nature, 2025).
